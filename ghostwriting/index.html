FAQ

How do you capture my voice accurately?
We analyze your best-performing posts — tone, sentence structure, emoji patterns, hooks, CTAs. The AI builds a voice profile that matches your style, not a generic "professional" tone. Most clients say the drafts sound like them on a good day.
What if I don't like a draft?
Edit it directly in the dashboard or reject it with a note. The AI learns from your edits. After the first week, draft approval rates are typically 80%+.
Do I have to post everything you generate?
No. You approve every single post. Nothing goes live without your green light. 15 min/week is all it takes to review a month's content.
What platforms do you support?
X/Twitter is fully supported. LinkedIn and Threads are available on Growth and Scale plans. We're adding more platforms based on client demand.
Is this just ChatGPT with a prompt?
No. We use a custom pipeline: voice profiling → topic engine → trending injection → draft generation → human review → auto-posting. The voice model is trained on YOUR content, not a generic template.
